Delicious Cupcake Recipe

Learn how to make the most delicious and fluffy cupcakes. Follow this simple recipe and impress your friends and family.

  • Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  • In a mixing bowl, combine 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour, 1 cup sugar, 1/2 cup unsalted butter (room temperature), 1/2 cup milk, 2 eggs, 1 teaspoon vanilla extract, and 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder.
  • Mix all ingredients until smooth.
  • Line a cupcake tin with paper liners and fill each cup 2/3 full with the batter.
  • Bake for 18-20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  • Let the cupcakes cool before frosting.

Enjoy your freshly made cupcakes!
